About Us

Thomas is a software developer and writer. So far he has written a little-played RPG called Sensational, an interactive play called Romans, and the first 8 episodes of a podcast called The Elandrid.

Epicatomic produces theater, games, stories, and miscellany.

CHARACTER-CENTRIC STORYTELLING

Epicatomic productions put characters first and foremost, focusing on the human experience, even under the most unusual or expansive circumstances.
